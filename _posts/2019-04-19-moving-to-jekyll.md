After many years of on and off dabbling with Wordpress blogs I have decided to make the move to Jekyll and Github Pages for hosting. When I started my career in IT ( too many years ago to recall now ) a lot of my time was spent developing PC applications firstly with [Smartware](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Informix_Corporation) then later with [Clipper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clipper_(programming_language)) and then small amounts of [Delphi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delphi_(software)) and web development using [ASP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_Server_Pages).

As my career developed I gradually moved away from programming and eventually ended up in an almost entirely management role. For some people this would have been their goal but my interests were always very much with the hands on elements and when I was given the opportunity (a euphamism!) to change paths a few years ago I was happy to find myself in a role where I could get back into the nitty gritty and use my skills and expertise in an area that I enjoyed rather than dreaded.
