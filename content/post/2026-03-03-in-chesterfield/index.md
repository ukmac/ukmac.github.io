Today marked the end of the main programme of work I was originally employed to work on in my current job. After three and a half years we have finally completed the transition to a single M365 tenant, a single IT support partner and a common Enterprise network (albeit that part is still two seperate infrastructures built to the same standard on the same Fortinet kit.)

After starting with half a dozen outsourced services we are now managing our IT service internally with the support of a primary IT partner, a field services partner and a managed security partner. 

Now starts the next phase of our journey to build on this base layer and start to leverage what we have to get us through Cyber Essentials+ certification.
