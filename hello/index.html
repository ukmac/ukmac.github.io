Hello

My Hello Page

Apr 19, 2024

Hello, I’m Scott Paterson.

Here is how I prefer to keep in touch, and why:

  • My favourite way to converse is email.
  • If you know me and have my phone number you can contact me on iMessage or Signal
  • I try to write on my website though not on a very regular basis
  • I have left Twitter or “X” because it is a cesspool.
  • I lurk on Mastodon and make the occasional comment or post
  • I am not a huge fan of LinkedIn, but use it to connect with work colleagues. I don’t use it for ongoing communication, I’d rather email.
  • I use Strava to publish my walks/rides and to check on my friends progress
  • I love Zwift and try to ride as often as I can
  • I have a Pixelfed account and really should use it more.
  • I don’t use Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp or Threads
